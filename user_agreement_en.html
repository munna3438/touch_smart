6. TAS Smart Services

TAS Smart Services record your training data and health data, which will be used only for your private and non-commercial purposes. Below are the main functions of TAS Smart Services.

  1. Account login: you need to create and log into your TAS Smart account, in order to manage and save your training data and health data.
  2. Device management: TAS Smart Services allow you to manage your device. You can manage the use and upgrade of your device by connecting your device to your phone through Bluetooth function. You can manage your personal data on your device and/or in the APP, which will be displayed after processed through the APP.
  3. Personal information management: you can edit your avatar, nickname, sex, age, height, weight and other information in the APP.
  4. Training data recording: TAS Smart Services support the recording and display of your daily training data (walking, running, biking, swimming, etc.) and movement path (outdoor running, outdoor walking, outdoor biking, etc.). To record your movement path, you need to enable the GPS function of your mobile phone.
  5. Health data management: After binding your device, TAS Smart Services will support the measurement, recording and display of your health data (sleep, heart rate, SpO2, stress, etc.).
  6. Medals: You will be awarded virtual medals, depending on your related training data and health data.
  7. Health reports: Generate your weekly, monthly and annual health reports based on your training data, health data and personal information, and push your health reports. Please note that these reports do not constitute professional medical advices.
  8. Functional services: TAS Smart provides additional functional services, including quick message reply, intelligent training identification, female menstrual cycle reminder, lifting wrist to turn on the screen, automatic brightness adjustment at night, multi-language switching, alarm clock, Find My Phone, music control and weather notification.
  9. Sharing: Share your weekly health report and other training data and health data to other social APPs on your phone.
  10. Questions and suggestions: If you encounter any problems in the course of use, you can edit and submit questions to us. If necessary, you can choose to upload logs.
  11. TAS Smart Services allow you to share data to third-party services, provided that you send us a sharing instruction.
  12. Purchase: TAS Smart provides virtual dial purchase service.
  13. Personalized ranking: Supports the statistics of personal information and sports data (personal avatar, nickname and sports data) of different types of people, and analyzes and ranks them.

Please note that we will update the APP or software from time to time, and the actual functions will depend on the version you are using.
