1. Information Collected

We may collect the following kinds of information when you use our products and services.

Account Information:

Creating an account on our application collects "required information" such as country or region, email address, password, date of birth, gender, height, weight, and "optional information" such as your nickname, weight goal, step goal, and profile picture. The information collected will be used as part of the health data algorithm. For example, the heart rate algorithm will generate relevant and more accurate health data based on your age, height, and weight. Your personal information will be anonymized and encrypted.

Exercise & Health-related Information:

steps, distance, speed, exercise time, calories, sleep time, heart rate, stress, oxygen saturation, walking time, exercise intensity, exercise type, ambient sound level, skin temperature, detailed exercise history for each exercise type, and medals (virtual medals awarded based on your exercise), etc. The exercise data collected will be used to provide you with personalized exercise feedback (e.g., evaluation of the effectiveness of your exercise) and to provide you with better health management services (e.g., developing good sleep habits). This type of data is encrypted and will not be collected if you opt out of the feature. Cloud data synchronization can be turned off in TAS Smart under "Data Analysis and Security".

Location Information:

 Your phone's location information may be used for certain location-based services. For example, it will be called up when you are in outdoor sports mode, checking the weather, or selecting your area. Such information collection will be disabled in the event you do not permit us to access your phone's GPS function.

Device MAC and APP Information:

your device and application information, such as user configuration data, device MAC address, device version number, serial number, firmware version, language, APP version, phone system version, device configuration, IP address, network type, device identification, etc., may be collected when you use our devices and applications. Only the information of the device that has been connected to our services will be obtained: when the APP is connected to the device, the information will be stored locally on the phone and uploaded to the server. This kind of information will be used for upgrade services such as updating the device firmware version, updating the APP version, updating the watch face version, updating the device language, or for locating and analyzing the relevant application or device firmware issues.

Service Information:

Certain features of TAS Smart devices and applications may access your personal information (e.g., reminder setting or exercise record). For example, the "Menstrual Cycle Reminder" feature may collect your menstrual cycle and ovulation data, etc.; the "Call Reminder” feature may access (but not store) your call status and contacts; the "Message Notification" feature may access (temporarily store) the notification status of third party applications; the "Music Control" feature may access (but not store) the information such as song playback and song status; and the "Alarm Clock" feature may use your phone's built-in clock function.

Feedback Information: 

 If you have any questions or suggestions, please give feedback through "My>Question and Suggestion", where you can provide your contact information at your discretion. Your feedback helps us better understand the problem you are experiencing. During the feedback process, if you agree, we may access your phone's camera, photo album, etc., so that you can upload pictures of the issue and help us quickly locate it.

Log Information: 

We may collect usage data when you use our devices or applications, which may include, for example, account creation or login information, account-device pairing information, device reboot information, network usage information, as well as information on system errors, crashes, reboots, and upgrades. Your latest log information within 7 days will be automatically stored locally on your phone while earlier log information will be automatically deleted. Uploading logs can help us resolve your issue at a faster rate. However, we do not gather such information unless you opt to upload your logs to our server through "My>Question and Suggestion". QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission:If you use the "Message Notification" and "Share" functions, you need to query the installed application list information. By default, the Android system platform only detects some filtered installed applications. You need to call the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission to get the list of installed applications on the device. The obtained app list information is only stored locally on your phone and will not be uploaded to our server.

Guest Information: 

 Users (who are not registered or logged in) can experience our products and certain limited services through the Guest feature. Guest personal data (nickname, gender, height, weight, date of birth, sports health data, etc.) will be stored locally on your phone and will not be uploaded to our servers.

Payment Information:

You may be required to provide your bank account information if you purchase products and/or services (e.g., from the Watch Face Market) within TAS Smart. This transaction information is collected and processed by third party payment service providers. TAS Smart may collect summary information about transactions, such as order information, but does not collect or store your bank account information. Android ID：If you use the Android system and use the app in the guest state,we will obtain your Android ID through the public API of the Android system,which will be used as a unique identifier for your use of the app.If the app is in the background,it will also be read when it needs to get the Android ID. Paid services: After logging in to your TAS Smart account, you can buy some services to obtain the corresponding rights and interests, virtual goods or assets. We will collect your payment records, membership status and rights, so that you can check them at any time. Your TAS Smart account information will be used to verify your identity, create and store your orders and collect your payment information. Meanwhile, it will be used to grant you the corresponding rights, virtual goods or assets, or share the payment status with third parties. Your virtual assets are sensitive data. We will take appropriate protective measures to ensure the accuracy and security of your asset data.

Other Information: 

We may collect other types of information that is not directly or indirectly linked to an individual, but is aggregated, anonymized or de-identified.

2. Usage of Information Collected

The information collected will be used to:

Provide, process, maintain, improve and develop our products and/or services, including after-sales, customer support and partial services on your device (e.g. use of heart rate data for heart rate algorithm optimization). All data is anonymized;

Communicate with you, as in using your information to send you service notifications when necessary, to reply to you when you contact us, or to push a reply message into your TAS Smart APP when you give feedback;

Store and maintain your information in accordance with our service offerings or legal obligations;

Provide you with limited services without communicating with our servers.

Use of Non-personal Data

TAS Smart collects non-personal data logs, such as error codes and logs of device reboots, error codes of hardware problems that cause device reboots, logs of Bluetooth connectivity of applications, device and application usage of anonymized users (opt-in), and more. The purpose is to perform an overall analysis to fix errors, monitor usage, and optimize our service. In addition, a third party log-capturing SDK (com.tencent.bugly) has been integrated for quick fixing of TAS Smart issues. These logs only contain information about the parameters of the user's phone and do not involve personal information.

4. Third Party Service Providers

We may rely on a third party to realize TAS Smart services and you can choose whether to use third-party services. When you agree to use third-party services, the third party may collect information about you. Therefore, we strongly encourage you to take the time to read the privacy policy of any third party providing services, just as you would read ours. We are not responsible or in control of how third parties use the personal information they collect from you. The following service providers have access to your personal data only to implement the TAS Smart service on our behalf and are obliged not to disclose it or use it for any other purpose:

Map Service Provider

In TAS Smart, user’s access to the outdoor single-exercise route is achieved through the SDKs of the following service providers: Amap (com.amap.api), Google (com.google.Maps) and Apple (com.apple.Maps). When you use the map service, your position information will be collected and provided to the service providers. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information on data collection and processing. The purpose of TAS Smart to obtain route data is to allow users to view routes after outdoor exercise. You can turn off GPS on your phone to terminate the collection of position information.

Weather Service Provider

If your device supports weather notifications, your location information will be sent locally to your weather service provider (IBM) for weather information in your area. You can stop this sharing at any time by turning off the feature in "Device > More > Weather Notifications". Your personal information is anonymized and will not be used to identify or locate you personally.

Cloud Storage Provider

The cloud storage provider for TAS Smart services is Amazon (Amazon Web Services EMEA SARL or Amazon Web Services, Inc.) When you select to store your data on the cloud, your personal data, including personal information, exercise data, and health data, will be uploaded to Amazon's servers. You may disable the "Cloud Sync" feature to stop uploading data to the cloud, or tap “Clear Cloud Data" to erase data that has been uploaded to the cloud. Please refer to Amazon's privacy policy for more information on data storage.

Alexa Voice Service Provider

TAS Smart's Alexa Voice Service is provided by Amazon (Amazon Web Services EMEA SARL or Amazon Web Services, Inc.) When you select to store your data on the cloud, your personal data will be uploaded to Amazon's servers. When available, your TAS Smart smart device will be logged into Amazon Alexa to enable voice features. You can log out of Alexa to terminate the voice service. Please refer to Amazon's privacy policy for more information on data storage.

Database Encryption Service Provider

Zetetic (net.zetetic:android-database-sqlcipher), a database encryption service provider, is available as part of the TAS Smart service. When stored locally, your data will be encrypted by this tool to avoid information leakage. Please refer to Zetetic's privacy policy for details on data encryption.

Tencent Bugly Service Provider

Tencent Bugly, a service provider (com.tencent.bugly，ios.bugly.qq.com) that collects abnormal states such as APP crashes and no response during use, facilitates more timely detection of control abnormalities, a more comprehensive understanding of positioning abnormalities, and a more efficient solution to user problems.Only obtain program exception data, without involving user-related personal information and data.

Payment Service Provider

Paid products and/or services may be offered within the TAS Smart APP. In such cases, we may use a third party service provider for payment processing (such as a payment application). Your payment details are not stored or collected by us, but are provided directly to our third party payment service providers whose use of your personal information is governed by their privacy policies.

Share SDK

Share SDK is a social sharing service provided by Mob Tech, which allows you to share information such as nickname, date, steps, etc. to third-party devices that support sharing. It will only be shared with your permission, otherwise it will not be shared. The terms of the privacy policy of this service can be referred to: https://www.mob.com/about/policy/en.

Third Party Login Instructions

In the TAS Smart service, third-party login authorization in WeChat, Facebook, and X social platform accounts is supported. When you use this service to log in and use TAS Smart, you will authorize us to obtain your public information (avatar, nickname, mobile phone number (encrypted)) registered on the third-party platform, which will be used to bind with the TAS Smart account, so that you can log in directly and use of this product and related services. You can also terminate the third-party login service by deleting the third-party binding. For specific data, please refer to the privacy policy of the relevant third party.
